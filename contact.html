Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to see results with the Anti-Spot Cream? ▼

Most users begin to see a visible softening of acne scars and minor dark spots within **14 days**. For stubborn hyperpigmentation, melasma, or sun damage, full clinical improvement is typically reached in **28 to 45 days** of consecutive daily application. Always combine with sunscreen in the morning!

Is it safe for pregnant or breastfeeding mothers? ▼

Our **Anti-Spot, Hydra-Boost, and Sunscreen formulas** are generally safe as they utilize mild ingredients like Niacinamide, Hyaluronic Acid, and Zinc oxide. However, our **Night Repair Cream** contains **Retinol**, which is contraindicated during pregnancy and breastfeeding. We recommend consulting your physician before starting any Retinol products.

How do you handle delivery inside Sri Lanka? ▼

We offer **island-wide delivery** across Sri Lanka. Orders inside Colombo are delivered within **1 to 2 business days**. Suburb and outstation deliveries (Kandy, Galle, Jaffna, etc.) take between **3 to 4 business days**. Best of all, shipping is 100% free on all cart items!
